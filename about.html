Our Process

Step 1: Initial Consultation

In the initial consultation, we take the time to understand your career goals, skills, and aspirations. Our team works closely with you to gather all the necessary information that will help us customize our services and guide you toward the most suitable opportunities in Germany. This step allows us to create a tailored plan that aligns with your ambitions and ensures that you receive the best support throughout your job search journey. Our goal is to lay a solid foundation for your successful career abroad.

Step 2: Job Search Assistance

Our job assistance services are designed to help you successfully land the right job in Germany. From identifying suitable job openings based on your qualifications to providing personalized support throughout the application process, we are committed to guiding you every step of the way. Our team connects you with top employers, ensures your resume and application stand out, and prepares you for interviews. With our expertise and extensive network, we increase your chances of securing a rewarding career abroad.

Step 3: Interview Preparation

We understand that the interview process can be daunting, especially when applying for positions in a foreign country. That’s why we offer thorough interview preparation to help you succeed. Our team will guide you through common interview questions, assist with tailoring your responses to the specific job role, and help you practice in mock interviews. Additionally, we provide insights into German workplace culture and expectations, ensuring you are well-prepared to impress potential employers. Our goal is to boost your confidence and equip you with the skills to excel during your interview.

Step 4: Visa Guidance

Navigating the visa process can be complex, but we are here to make it simpler for you. Our team provides comprehensive support in preparing and submitting your visa applications, ensuring all necessary documentation is in order. We guide you through the different types of visas available for working in Germany, explain the requirements, and help you stay on track with deadlines. With our expert assistance, you can be confident that your visa process will be smooth and hassle-free, allowing you to focus on your career journey in Germany.

Step 5: Relocation Support

We understand that moving to a new country can be both exciting and overwhelming. Our relocation support services are designed to make your transition to Germany as smooth as possible. From finding suitable housing to assisting with local registration, setting up bank accounts, and navigating cultural differences, we ensure that every aspect of your move is handled with care. Our team is here to guide you through the relocation process, so you can focus on settling into your new role and life in Germany with confidence.

Step 6: Post-Placement Support

At Saanvik Global, our support doesn't end once you've secured a job. We understand that transitioning to a new role in a new country can be challenging, so we provide continuous assistance even after your placement. Whether it’s adjusting to the work culture, addressing concerns about your accommodation, or simply navigating life in Germany, our team is here to ensure that you settle in smoothly. We are committed to your long-term success, providing ongoing guidance to ensure your career and personal growth in Germany continues to flourish.
